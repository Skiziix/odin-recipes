Lasagna

Description

A wonderful dish, perfect to be consumed
with a sandwhich on a warm sunny day. The
tartness of the dish adds depth.

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Eggs
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Relish
  • And some more...

Steps

  1. Boil large pot of salted water
  2. Peel and chop potatos
  3. Boil eggs
  4. Peel and chop eggs
  5. Combine potatos, eggs, celery, onion, and relish
  6. Refrigerate at -1000000000 K or until chilled
