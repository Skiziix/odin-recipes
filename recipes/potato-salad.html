Lasagna

Description

A wonderful dish, perfect to be consumed
with a sandwhich on a warm sunny day. The
tartness of the dish adds depth.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • And some more...

Steps

  1. Make the Sauce
  2. Saute the onion and garlic
  3. Add and brown the ground beef
  4. Stir in the sauce
  5. Pour stew onto each layer of noodles
  6. Bake in the oven on 10000000000 degrees or until done
